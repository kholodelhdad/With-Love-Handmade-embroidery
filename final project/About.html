A Labor of Love
So much tender loving care goes into the creation of each embroidery design you will find here---from carefully chosen cheery colors, precisely punched designs, or various appealing textures and fills. Designs are our passion.
We take pride in our designs and we love it when you share your embroidery with us. From nursery rhymes to holiday themes, our work aims to capture the spirit of all of your moods, moments and memories.
Thank you for visiting our website. We look forward to seeing what you create with our designs! We have been adding tutorial videos to ourYouTube Channel for our new in the hoop projects!
